Fishing Skills

There are multiple ways to catch fish but the most popular is casting. Anyone with a rod and reel can cast near or on any body of water. Boats are not needed for this method. The other method is trolling. This method uses a down rigger on a boat to sink lures beneath the surface to attact fish while the boat is moving.


Spot #1

Michigan Center Lake

This is my favorite shore fishing spot back in my hometown. At this spot I user the carolina rig method to catch carp. There is a carp carnival in the nearby town every June because of how many carp in in this lake.

Spot #2

Turner Park Lansing

This is my favorite spot to go fishing at while at school. This may not be the best spot but I am always able to catch a nice sized smallmouth bass while fishing with minnows here.

Spot #3

Au Sable River

This is my all time favorite secret spot. This place is pretty desolate and is only attracted to by locals. In this spot there are monster steelhead and even some walleye to catch downstream. In this spot I use a more advanced fly fishing technique.
